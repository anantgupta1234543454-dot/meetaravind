What is Aravind

Not a therapist.
Not a guru.
Something else.

Aravind is an AI spiritual guide grounded in centuries of Indian philosophical inquiry. It draws from the Upanishads, the Bhagavad Gita, and the teachings of India's great philosophical traditions to offer honest, direct, and warm guidance on the questions that actually matter — purpose, identity, relationships, and the nature of the mind.

It is not a religious service. It is not a wellness app. It is a thoughtful conversational guide that meets you where you are and asks the questions worth sitting with.

"You are not suffering your life — you are suffering your memory of the past and your imagination of the future."

Privacy Policy

Your data.
Your conversation.

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Aravind Spirituality ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Aravind WhatsApp service.

1. Information We Collect

When you send a message to Aravind via WhatsApp, we receive the content of your message and your WhatsApp phone number. We store conversation history on our servers solely to provide continuity of the service. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information unless you choose to share it in a conversation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your messages solely to generate responses from Aravind and to improve the quality of the service. We may review anonymised conversation excerpts internally. We never use individual conversations for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or conversation history with any third party, except as required by applicable law. We use the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. to deliver this service.

4. Data Retention

Conversation history is stored on our servers for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing meetaravind.ai@gmail.com. We will delete all stored conversation data associated with your number within 7 business days.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage and access controls.

6. Children

This service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
